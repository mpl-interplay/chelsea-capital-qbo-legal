Privacy Policy

This privacy policy describes how we handle data within our internal automation app hosted on n8n under the Interplay account. The app is used exclusively to connect to Chelsea Capital’s QuickBooks Online (QBO) account to interact with account data for operational purposes.

Data Collected

We collect and store OAuth tokens provided by Intuit to authenticate with QuickBooks Online. These tokens are used solely to interact with Chelsea Capital’s QBO account data via the QuickBooks API.

Usage

The app is used internally to support operational workflows by accessing account data from Chelsea Capital’s QuickBooks account. It is not intended for public or commercial use.

Data Sharing

No data accessed or processed by the app is shared with any third party.

Security

OAuth tokens are stored securely within n8n’s encrypted credential vault, accessible only by authorized personnel at Interplay.
